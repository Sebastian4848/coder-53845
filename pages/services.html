Services

Below are the primary services we offer. For custom solutions tailored to your specific needs, please contact us. Our sales representatives will be pleased to assist you.

Mechanical Systems 3D Modeling

Experience precision and efficiency with our comprehensive 3D modeling service. From ventilation and hydronic systems to fire protection and medical gas systems, we specialize in creating accurate digital representations of complex mechanical systems, ensuring seamless integration and optimal performance in your projects.

Electrical Systems 3D Modeling

Experience unparalleled precision and efficiency with our specialized 3D modeling service for electrical systems. We provide highly detailed digital representations of main distribution systems, intricate lighting layouts, and conduits. This ensures seamless integration, optimal design, and enhanced project visualization.

MEP BIM Coordination

Halo Mechanical Building Services offers expert MEP BIM Coordination, ensuring seamless integration of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems. Our precise modeling and clash detection optimize efficiency and functionality for complex projects like hospitals and data centers.

Mechanical Shop Drawings

Halo Mechanical Building Services provides precise mechanical shop drawings tailored for complex construction projects. Specializing in hospitals, data centers, and industrial plants, our detailed drawings ensure accurate fabrication and installation, optimizing efficiency and adherence to specifications.

Electrical Shop Drawings

Halo Mechanical Building Services delivers precise electrical shop drawings for complex projects. Specializing in hospitals, data centers, and industrial plants, our detailed drawings ensure accurate installation of electrical systems, enhancing efficiency, compliance, and project success.

Dynamo Scripting

Halo Mechanical Building Services offers expert Dynamo scripting services to automate and enhance BIM workflows. Specializing in complex projects like hospitals, data centers, and industrial plants, we create custom scripts to optimize efficiency, streamline processes, and ensure project accuracy.
