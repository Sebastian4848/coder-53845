About Us

Who we are

Halo Mechanical Building Services is Chilean based contruction services company focused on BIM Methodology. We focus on providing solutions for contruction sites that need precise and high quality shop drawings for accurate and clash free intallation of mechanical systems. Our goal is to provide high quality services with the aim of reducing delays and cost in the contrucction process. Our experience revolves around high complexity installations like Hospitals, Datacenter and Water Processing Plants.

Our History

Halo Mechanical Building Services was founded in Santiago de Chile in 2020 given the local need for specialized mechanical contractors in the region, the founder of HMBS is Sebastian Ojeda, Master in Mechanical Engineering fom Politecnico di Milano. Soon after it's creation the company adquired several contracts to provide contruction services for a Hospital and several smaller industries in Santiago, given the sucess of the initial contracts HMBS expanded to Argentina where is currently working in the Buenos Aires Water Treatment Plant expansion project.
